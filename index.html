About

When I was first introduced to the world of computer science, I was a young undergraduate student. My passion for problem solving led me to earn a degree in mathematics. But when my degree plan led me to computer science, I was excited to explore these new ways to problem solve! Web and software development soon became my favorite ways to practice and apply everything I learned. It was this spirit that inspired the creation of Vedev.


Here, we are passionate about delivering the best development practices to support others on their creative journey. Whether that involves a modern website to present your best to the world or the innovative application you’ve always envisioned, Vedev is here to help you Summon Success.

- Valeria Felder, Creator
